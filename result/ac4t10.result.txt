### Questions ###

Question 1: What was the main ethical issue in YouTuber T.D.’s charity video?

Question 2: What message does this case send about intention in charitable acts?

Question 3: What lesson can content creators and the public learn from this incident?




### Answers ###

Question 1: The charity was used for personal gain views and popularity rather than to genuinely help others, turning giving into public humiliation.

Question 2: It shows that without sincere intention, charity can become performative and harmful, emphasizing that ethics matter as much as actions.

Question 3: Giving should never come at the cost of another’s dignity. Ethical content creation means respecting the people featured, especially when they are in need.
